Chee$eCake Recipe

IngredientS

  1. For the Base!
    • 200g digestive biscuits
    • 100g unsalted butter, melted
  2. For the Cheesecake Filling!
    • 600g cream cheese, at room temperature
    • 250g caster sugar
    • 200ml sour cream
    • 3 large eggs
    • 1 tsp vanilla extract
    • 1 tbsp plain flour

InstructionS

  1. Prepare the Base!
    • Crush the digestive biscuits into fine crumbs.
    • Mix the melted butter with the biscuit crumbs until well combined.
    • Press the mixture firmly into the base of a 23cm (9 inch) springform tin.
    • Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the Oven!
    • Preheat your oven to 160°C (320°F).
  3. Make the Cheesecake Filling
    • In a large mixing bowl, beat the cream cheese until smooth.
    • Add the caster sugar and continue to beat until combined.
    • Add the sour cream, eggs, vanilla extract, and plain flour. Mix until smooth.
  4. Bake the Cheesecake!
    • Pour the cheesecake filling over the chilled biscuit base.
    • Smooth the top with a spatula.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set but still has a slight wobble in the center.
    • Bake in the preheated oven for about 50-60 minutes, or until the cheesecake is set but still has a slight wobble in the center.
  5. Chill the Cheesecake!
    • Once the cheesecake has cooled to room temperature, chill it in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.
  6. Serve
    • Remove the cheesecake from the tin.
    • Top with fresh berries or fruit compote, if desired.
    • Slice and enjoy!
