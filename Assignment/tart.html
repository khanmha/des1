BakeWell Tart Recipe

IngredientS

  1. For the pastry!
    • 200g plain flour
    • 2 tbsp icing sugar
    • 100g cold butter, cubed
    • 1 egg yolk
    • 2-3 tbsp cold water
  2. For the Filling!
    • 150g raspberry jam
    • 150g ground almonds
    • 150g butter, softened
    • 3 eggs
    • 150g caster sugar
    • 1 tsp almond extract
    • 50g flaked almonds

InstructionS

  1. Prepare the Pastry!
    • Mix flour and icing sugar in a bowl.
    • Rub in the butter until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs.
    • Add egg yolk and cold water, mix to form a dough.
    • Wrap in cling film and chill for 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat the Oven!
    • Preheat to 180°C (160°C fan)/350°F/gas mark 4.
  3. Roll out the pastry!
    • Roll out the chilled pastry on a floured surface.
    • Line a 23cm tart tin with the pastry, trim the edges.
    • Prick the base with a fork and chill for 10 minutes.
  4. Blind bake the Pastry!
    • Line the pastry with baking paper and fill with baking beans.
    • Bake for 15 minutes, remove beans and paper, bake for another 5 minutes.
  5. Prepare the filling!
    • Spread raspberry jam over the base of the pastry.
    • Cream butter and sugar together until light and fluffy.
    • Beat in the eggs one at a time, then add almond extract.
    • Fold in the ground almonds.
  6. Assemble the tart!
    • Pour the almond mixture over the jam and spread evenly.
    • Sprinkle flaked almonds on top.
  7. Bake the tart!
    • Bake for 35-40 minutes until golden and set.
    • Cool in the tin before serving.
