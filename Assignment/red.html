Red Velvet Cake Recipe

IngredientS

  1. Cake!
    • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour.
    • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar.
    • 1 teaspoon baking soda.
    • 2 large eggs, room temperature.
    • 1 teaspoon salt.
    • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder.
    • 1 1/2 cups vegetable oil.
    • 1 cup buttermilk, room temperature.
    • 2 tablespoons red food coloring.
    • 1 cup hot water.
    • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract.
    • 1 teaspoon white vinegar.
  2. Frosting!
    • 1 pound (2 cups) cream cheese, softened.
    • 1/2 pound (2 sticks) butter, softened.
    • 4 cups powdered sugar, sifted.
    • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract.

InstructionS

  1. Preheat and Prepare!
    • Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
    • Grease and flour two 9-inch round cake pans.
  2. Dry Ingredients!
    • In a medium bowl, sift together flour, sugar, baking soda, salt, and cocoa powder.
  3. Wet Ingredients!
    • In a large bowl, gently beat together the vegetable oil, buttermilk, eggs, food coloring, vanilla, and vinegar using an electric mixer.
  4. Combine!
    • Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, and mix until just combined.
    • Gradually add the hot water, mixing until smooth.
  5. Bake!
    • Divide the batter evenly between the prepared pans.
    • Bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
    • Cool the cakes in the pans for 10 minutes, then remove them from the pans to cool completely on a wire rack.
  6. Frosting
    • In a large bowl, beat the cream cheese and butter until smooth.
    • Gradually add the powdered sugar, beating until light and fluffy.
    • Mix in the vanilla extract.
  7. Assemble
    • Place one cake layer on a serving plate.
    • Spread a generous layer of frosting on top.
    • Place the second cake layer on top and frost the top and sides of the cake.
    • Serve
      • Decorate as desired and serve!
